Features

Everything a TA Needs

📊

Marks Management

Enter student marks directly on the portal and watch them automatically sync to your Google Sheet in real-time. No manual export needed.

🔗

Student Invite Link

Generate a unique link for your class. Students click it, see your name and Sir's name, and enter their own data — Roll No., name, and more.

✅

Permission Control

You decide who can join your class. Review and approve or reject students who register via your link before they're added to your roster.

🔔

Absence Reminders

Automatically notify students who are absent or have missed assignments. Stay on top of attendance without extra effort.

🗄️

Secure Backend Storage

All student data, marks, and class records are securely stored in the cloud using Supabase — accessible anytime, anywhere.

👤

TA Profile

Your name and your Sir's name are displayed on the student portal page so students always know exactly whose class they're joining.

Workflow

How It Works

1

TA Signs Up

Create your account, add your name and Sir's name to set up your profile.

2

Generate Class Link

Get a unique shareable link for your class and send it to your students.

3

Students Register

Students open the link, see TA & Sir details, and enter their Roll No. and name.

4

TA Approves

Review registrations and approve only students belonging to your class.

5

Manage & Sync

Enter marks on the portal — they instantly sync to your Google Sheet automatically.
